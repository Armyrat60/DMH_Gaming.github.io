Our Community

Hey there, fellow gamers! We're thrilled to have you join our growing community of gaming enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just getting started, you're in the right place.

At DMH, we're all about one thing: having a blast while playing Squad and other exciting games together. We've come together to create a tight-knit group of friends with the same gaming passion.

Our community focuses on creating an inclusive, friendly environment where players can enjoy tactical gameplay while making lasting friendships. We offer:

  • Regular community events and tournaments
  • Dedicated servers with custom configurations
  • Active Discord community with voice channels
  • Training sessions for new players
  • Organized clan matches and operations

Our Name's Origin

19TH CENTURY LEGEND

"Wild Bill" Hickok's legendary tale in the American Old West came to its dramatic end in 1876, within a Deadwood saloon. A skilled gunslinger and lawman, Hickok met his fate during a poker game, holding what would become known as the "dead man's hand": two pairs of black aces and eights.
